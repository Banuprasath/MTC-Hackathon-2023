About Carbon Footprint

Your carbon footprint is a measure of the total amount of greenhouse gases, primarily carbon dioxide (CO2), that are released into the atmosphere as a result of your daily activities and lifestyle choices. These activities include energy consumption, transportation, waste production, and more. Understanding your carbon footprint is important for identifying areas where you can reduce your environmental impact and contribute to a more sustainable future.

By calculating and making conscious efforts to reduce your carbon footprint, you can help mitigate the effects of climate change, conserve natural resources, and create a healthier planet for future generations.

Use our Carbon Footprint Calculator to assess the environmental impact of your car and household activities, and learn how you can take steps to minimize your contribution to greenhouse gas emissions.
